Begin Again — Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 23, 2026

Overview

Begin Again ("the App") is designed to help you break negative thought loops and take small, meaningful actions. Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

Begin Again collects no personal information. We do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or any other identifying information.

Usage Data

The App tracks your loop-breaking activity (number of prompts completed) locally on your device and, if enabled, syncs this data via Apple's iCloud Key-Value Storage to your other personal devices. This data is not accessible to us and is stored entirely within your personal iCloud account.

Subscriptions

Begin Again offers an optional auto-renewable subscription managed entirely through Apple's App Store. Payment information is handled by Apple and is never shared with us. For details on how Apple handles your payment data, please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The App does not contain ads.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer any user data to third parties. There is no data to share because we do not collect any.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the revised version will be posted at this URL with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at:
