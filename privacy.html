BeThere — Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 23, 2026

Overview

BeThere ("the App") helps you stay connected with the people who matter. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

BeThere stores data you enter (contacts, reminders, notes) locally on your device and, if enabled, syncs via your personal iCloud account. We do not have access to this data.

Permissions

The App may request access to your Contacts and Notifications to provide its core functionality. This data is used only within the App and is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The App does not contain ads.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer any user data to third parties.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the revised version will be posted at this URL with an updated effective date.
